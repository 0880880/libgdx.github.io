MicroMania!
RedSponge, איתמרשמלו

Description
Play as Mici The Microwave in an auto-scroller where you use the power of Heat, Light and Electricity to reach the farthest point possible! Controls: Arrows to move, Space to jump, Z, X and C to use energy abilities.
